MEET-UPS


Our ultimate goal is to get our own place to call "home",
but for now, we play every week at the following locations:


  • Dragon's Den every Thursday from 5:30-8:30 PM
  • VP Pub & Grill every Saturday from 12:00-5:00 PM

Note: Both venues can be accessed by people who use wheelchairs.


COST: free


TOURNAMENTS


Playing in tournaments is one of the best ways to improve your game, so we want to make sure players have lots of opportunities to play in both unrated and rated tournaments within Saskatchewan.
We hold:


  • Periodic mock/practice tournaments
  • Weekly blitz tournaments
  • Monthly rapid tournaments
  • 3-5 classical tournaments per year
  • Variety of online tournaments

Check our calendar for tournament dates.

COST: varies*



WORKSHOPS


Education is a major core of our organization. Check back later to see the workshops we will offer this year or send us an e-mail to be added to our Workshop list.


COST: varies*

COACHING


We offer personalized, one-on-one coaching. We will meet with you for a consultation to see what level you play at, your style, and get a sense of what you would like to improve. We will then match you with one of our coaches that best fits what you are looking for.


COST: at the coach's discretion
