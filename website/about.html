WHAT WE PLAY FOR:

MISSION STATEMENT


To promote the educational value of chess, as well as grow and sustain a thriving community of players in Saskatchewan by providing opportunities to learn and play competitively or casually through affordable and accessible workshops, coaching, and tournaments.


VISION


A province where chess players find empowerment through

  1. challenging and rewarding competition,
  2. being part of a network of supportive mentors and peers, and
  3. developing an excellence in critical thinking, inner strength, and creativity.
