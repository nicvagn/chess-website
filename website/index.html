Welcome to the Saskatchewan Horizon Chess Club


Our organization is made up of diverse individuals who love chess in their own way: some of us compete, others play casually, while the rest just love watching the game. What ties us together is a collective dream to build a thriving chess community in Saskatchewan and the belief that the game offers something great for everyone.
