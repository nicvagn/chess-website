Saskatchewan Horizon Chess Club Mission




MISSION STATEMENT

To promote the educational value of chess, as well as grow and sustain a thriving community of players in Saskatchewan by providing opportunities to learn and play competitively or casually through affordable and accessible workshops, coaching, and tournaments.


VISION STATEMENT

A province where chess players find empowerment through challenging and rewarding competition, being part of a network of supportive mentors and peers, and developing an excellence in critical thinking, inner strength, and creativity.
