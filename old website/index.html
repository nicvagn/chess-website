Sask Chess


Mission Statement

Promoting chess throughout Saskatchewan, not only out of our love of the game, but in recognition of the benefits it brings to others in various areas like critical thinking, inner strength, and creativity. To grow chess in Saskatchewan by providing opportunities for group workshops, coaching, and competition, to increase awareness of the educational value of chess. Our mission is to expand and enhance the chess culture and community in Saskatoon, and provide a place to experience the game. We do this through providing a safe place for fostering new players’ chess journey, as well as organizing programs and events in pursuit of this aim. We do this through expanding the opportunities for chess players, providing programs and tournaments, and fostering players’ pursuit of their chess goals.
